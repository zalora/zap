Their Goal


With the increase in competition and influx of new brands, Stuhrling understood the need to utilize ZAP’s paid package to reach a larger audience. This includes running campaigns on social media and third party platforms such as Google to drive higher traffic directly to their ZALORA shopping page and boost sales conversion.

The Solution

Stuhrling was able to grasp the attention of ZALORA’s fashion-focused target audience on social media: Facebook and Instagram. This allowed Stuhrling to reach a greater pool of customers and increase their brand awareness. Visitors who clicked on the posts would be brought to Stuhrling’s shopping page on ZALORA directly, improving its web traffic and visits significantly.

SERVICES USED

Paid Marketing

Promote traffic and sales conversion to your brands’ online store on ZALORA through third party platforms such as Facebook and Google. This allows your advertisements to extend reach beyond ZALORA’s platform, to target new customers or retarget existing customers.

The Result

With Stuhrling’s campaigns running on ZALORA’s social media and third party platforms, the ZALORA team managed to expand reach and grasp the attention of potential customers. This resulted in a remarkable leap in impressions and sales.
